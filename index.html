The Trials and Tribulations of a Travleing Germaphobe

Arizona Bus

In 2017 I went on a day trip from Las Vegas to the Grand Canyon. I rode on a bus that took about 4 hours to get to the Canyon, so we only had a brief amount of time at each "Canyon Stop". One of those stops featured a famous ice cream shop right on the edge of the canyon, but the line was so long that I didn't have time to get any!! When I was back on the bus, the little girl who sat next to me had some. Her parents, cousins, uncles, etc. were all sitting around us, licking their ice creams. I was kind of grossed out when I noticed the girl passing her cone around for each family memebr to get a licking from it. Ew! When I told her the ice cream looked good, and told her I didn't have any time to buy some, she shoved her cone towards me and asked, "Do you want some?"

Icelandic Plane

I thought I had survived Iceland without an incident. I thought wrong. The plane had just landed on American soil. I stood up from my seat and started stretching. To my dismay, I saw an elderly man to my right picking his nose. He didn't just pick his nose, though. You could hear the liquids in his nose as he moved his finger around inside his nostrils. He then proceeded to eat his bougers. He did this about three or four times; just enough to make me almost vomit. Think about this next time you have to touch one of those seatbelts in an airplane. ;)
